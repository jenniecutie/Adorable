Puto

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 30 mins
Total: 50 mins
Servings: 18

Ingredients/ Direction to cook/

Description:

Puto is a steamed rice cake delicacy served as a snack in the Philippines. Puto is frequently paired with savory foods like dinuguan (pork blood stew) or pancit (rice noodle). The traditional one is made with stone-ground rice, water, and sugar to produce the batter, which we call 'galapong.' Normally, they are fermented overnight before being steamed. When rice flour became available, everything got much easier.
