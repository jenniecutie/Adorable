Description/ Ingredients/


1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.

2. Combine rice flour, baking powder, and salt then mix well. Set aside.

3. Cream butter then gradually put-in sugar while whisking. Add the eggs then whisk until every ingredient is well incorporated. Gradually add the rice flour, salt, and baking powder mixture then continue mixing. Pour-in coconut milk and fresh milk then whisk some more for 1 to 2 minutes.

4. Arrange the pre-cut banana leaf on a cake pan or baking pan.
