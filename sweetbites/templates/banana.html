Banana Cue

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Total: &15 mins
Servings: 3

Ingredients/ Direction to cook

Description:

The term "banana cue" refers to fried skewered fruit cooked with brown sugar. This is a Filipino delicacy that is typically eaten as a mid-afternoon snack. Banana Cue is one of the most popular street foods in the Philippines, and it can readily be found on the streets of Manila and other parts of the country.
