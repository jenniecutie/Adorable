Description:

Turon refers to a Filipino snack. It's also known as banana lumpia or deep-fried banana buns. Turon, a traditional Filipino meryenda and dessert, has grown in popularity over time. This sweet food is frequently seen being sold on the streets alongside bananacue or even vegetarian lumpia. It's simple to make and the materials are cheap.
